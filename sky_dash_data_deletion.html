Data Deletion Instructions — Sky Dash

Last updated: 2025-08-21

Sky Dash does not store personal data on its own servers. If you played via Meta Instant Games (Facebook), you can remove Sky Dash and associated data by following the steps below.

  1. Open Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings.
  2. Go to Apps and Websites.
  3. Find Sky Dash and choose Remove.
  4. Confirm removal and choose the option to delete posts, videos or events the app posted on your behalf (if any).

You may also email a deletion request to subroto16.debnath@gmail.com with the subject “Sky Dash Data Deletion Request” and include your Facebook profile link or User ID. We will coordinate deletion of any remaining data accessible to us via the platform.
